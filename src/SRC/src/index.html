Inception follows Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in stealing secrets from dreams. He is tasked with planting an idea in someone's subconscious through a process called "inception." As Cobb and his team navigate layers of dreams, his personal traumas threaten the mission. The film explores the line between dreams and reality.

Interstellar follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet. The film explores themes of love, time, and the survival of humanity. Interstellar is known for its stunning visuals and emotional storytelling.

Titanic follows the story of Jack and Rose, two passengers on the ill-fated RMS Titanic. The film explores themes of love, class, and tragedy. Titanic is known for its epic romance and historical accuracy.

Scarface follows Tony Montana, a Cuban immigrant who rises to power in Miami's drug trade. Consumed by ambition and paranoia, his empire spirals into chaos. His rise and fall paint a cautionary tale of power and greed.

Baywatch follows a team of lifeguards who protect the beaches of Los Angeles. The film is known for its action, humor, and iconic slow-motion running scenes. Baywatch is a fun and entertaining movie that pays homage to the original TV series.

The Dark Knight follows Batman as he faces the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to create chaos in Gotham City. As the Joker challenges Batman’s morals and pushes him to his limits, Gotham’s fate hangs in the balance. The film explores themes of justice, sacrifice, and the fine line between hero and vigilante.

Avatar follows Jake Sully, a paraplegic marine who takes on a mission to infiltrate the Na'vi people on the planet Pandora. As Jake learns about the Na'vi and their connection to the planet, he must choose between loyalty to humanity and his newfound family. The film is known for its groundbreaking visual effects and environmental message.

The Butler tells the story of Cecil Gaines, an African American man who serves as a butler in the White House for over three decades. As he witnesses pivotal moments in U.S. history, the film explores his personal struggles, the civil rights movement, and his relationship with his family. It’s a powerful look at race, power, and change in America.

Braveheart follows William Wallace, a Scottish warrior who leads a rebellion against the English king Edward I. As Wallace fights for freedom and independence, he becomes a symbol of hope for his people. The film is known for its epic battles, emotional storytelling, and themes of courage and sacrifice.

The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man wrongfully imprisoned for murder, and his friendship with fellow inmate Red. Over the years, Andy maintains hope and finds a way to escape, demonstrating the power of resilience and the importance of human connection. The film is a profound exploration of hope, justice, and redemption.

A Silent Voice follows Shoya Ishida, a boy who bullies a deaf classmate, Shoko Nishimiya, in elementary school. Years later, Shoya seeks redemption by reconnecting with Shoko, exploring themes of bullying, guilt, forgiveness, and the impact of human connection. The film is an emotional journey of growth and understanding.

Kimi no Na wa is a 2016 Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story follows two teenagers, Mitsuha and Taki, who mysteriously swap bodies across time and space. As they try to navigate each other's lives, they develop a deep connection, despite never having met. The film explores themes of love, fate, and memory, all while featuring stunning animation and a poignant soundtrack. Ultimately, it’s a tale about the unbreakable bond between the two, even when separated by time and distance.

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 follows Yuta Okkotsu, a high school student who gains cursed powers after a tragic event. He enrolls in the Tokyo Jujutsu High School to learn how to control his powers and fight curses. The film is a prequel to the Jujutsu Kaisen anime series and explores themes of friendship, loss, and the battle between good and evil.

Flow follows a group of friends who discover a mysterious power that allows them to manipulate time. As they experiment with their abilities, they uncover dark secrets and face dangerous consequences. The film is a thrilling sci-fi adventure that explores the consequences of playing with time.

Princess Mononoke tells the story of Ashitaka, a young prince who is cursed by a boar demon and embarks on a quest to find a cure. Along the way, he encounters Princess Mononoke, a human raised by wolves, and is caught in the conflict between nature spirits and human industrialization. The film explores themes of environmentalism, the consequences of human progress, and the balance between nature and civilization.
