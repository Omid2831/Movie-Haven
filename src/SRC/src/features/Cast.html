What happens on Younger?

*Younger* is a charming dramedy about reinvention, secrets, and second chances. Liza Miller (Sutton Foster), a recently divorced 40-something, is determined to relaunch her career in New York City's competitive publishing world. But when her age becomes a barrier, a serendipitous meeting with 26-year-old Josh (Nico Tortorella)—who assumes she’s his age—sparks an audacious plan. Encouraged by her quirky best friend Maggie (Debi Mazar), Liza decides to pose as a twentysomething to land an entry-level position at Empirical Press. Balancing her fresh start, a budding romance with Josh, and the challenges of keeping her true age a secret, Liza builds meaningful connections, including a close friendship with her ambitious coworker Kelsey (Hilary Duff) and a growing bond with the company’s charismatic executive (Peter Hermann). *Younger* is the perfect pick for fans of feel-good series with heartfelt moments, comedic twists, and empowering messages about chasing your dreams—no matter where you are in life.
