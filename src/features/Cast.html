WHERE WAS TRAIN YOUR DRAGON FILMED?

The live-action adaptation of "How to Train Your Dragon," set for release on June 13, 2025, was primarily filmed in Northern Ireland. Key locations included Belfast, where studio work was conducted at Titanic Studios and Belfast Harbour Studios, as well as on-location shoots in Tollymore Forest Park, County Down. Additionally, some filming took place in Los Angeles, California.

  • Mason Thames

    Biography:
    Mason Thames is a rising star in Hollywood, known for his captivating performances and versatility. He gained widespread recognition for his role in the critically acclaimed film "The Black Phone," where he showcased his exceptional acting skills. With a promising career ahead, Mason continues to impress audiences with his dedication and talent.

    Notable Works:
    Mason Thames, at 17, is already a rising star. He recently starred in "The Black Phone" with Ethan Hawke, directed by Scott Derrickson. He also filmed the lead in "How to Train Your Dragon" and starred in Movie Haven's exclusive "Incoming". Thames will appear in "Monster Summer" alongside Mel Gibson, directed by David Henrie.   • Gerard Butler

    Biography:
    Gerard Butler is a renowned Scottish actor and film producer, known for his dynamic roles and commanding screen presence. He gained international fame for his portrayal of King Leonidas in the epic film "300." With a career spanning over two decades, Butler has showcased his versatility in a wide range of genres, from action-packed blockbusters to heartfelt dramas.

    Notable Works:
    Gerard Butler's journey in the entertainment industry began with a law degree, but his passion for acting led him to the stage and eventually to Hollywood. He has starred in numerous successful films, including "The Phantom of the Opera," "Olympus Has Fallen," and "How to Train Your Dragon." Butler's dedication to his craft and his ability to embody diverse characters have made him a beloved figure in the film industry.

  • Dean DeBlois

    Biography:
    Dean DeBlois is a Canadian film director, producer, screenwriter, and editor. He is best known for co-writing and co-directing the animated feature films "Lilo & Stitch" and the "How to Train Your Dragon" trilogy.
